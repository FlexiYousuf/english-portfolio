Learning Activity 1.1

Communication Skills

I can help others feel comfortable by putting down any distractions and paying full attention when they are speaking. I can also react to the conversation using non-verbal gestures like nodding while listening, and bringing up common topics we both enjoy, like a show, video game, or sport.

My Learning Goals

Goal 1: Connections

One learning goal I will work on is to make meaningful connections between what I read and the world around me. I can do this by connecting class readings to real-world issues or my own experiences.

Goal 2: Point of View

Another goal I will work on is to recognize and question the point of view in different texts. I can practice this by looking at who is telling the story and why they are sharing that specific perspective.

Goal 3: Effective Expression

A third goal is to express my ideas more effectively and confidently when writing and speaking. I will work on this by organizing my assignments clearly and participating more in discussions and argument skills.

Goal 4: Reading Complex Texts

A fourth goal is to expand my active reading strategies when exploring deep novels. I plan to focus on breaking down advanced vocabulary, tracking subplots, and reflecting on themes to build strong critical comprehension.
